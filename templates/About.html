About VanSuraksha

VanSuraksha is an AI-powered real-time forest fire and smoke detection platform built to protect India's rich biodiversity. Our system enables early-stage wildfire detection, alerts first responders, and provides actionable intelligence to minimize damage to lives and ecosystems.

The platform integrates cutting-edge deep learning models, satellite fire data, and live CCTV monitoring to deliver a comprehensive forest protection suite.

  • Drone-based and CCTV surveillance for fire ignition confirmation
  • AI fire detection module identifying specific fire and smoke patterns
  • Real-time alert system with image capture and timestamps
  • MODIS satellite data integration for wildfire trend forecasting
  • User-friendly dashboard for wildfire management and monitoring

Our Mission

What VanSuraksha Is Doing

Healthy forest ecosystems are critical for our communities and climate action. Forests are the lungs of our earth — VanSuraksha is working to protect and restore them.

Forests Are Root of Life

India has over 71 million hectares of forest cover, supporting biodiversity, livelihoods, and climate regulation. Protecting forests protects life itself.

Deforestation Devastation

Deforestation triggers erosion, climate change, and loss of biodiversity. Forest fires in India contribute significantly to tree cover loss each year.

Combat Climate Change

Forests absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen. When destroyed, they release stored carbon — worsening the climate crisis. VanSuraksha ensures early intervention.
