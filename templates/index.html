About VanSuraksha

Guarding India's Green Heritage

India's forests span over 71 million hectares — 21.7% of the country's total land area. Each year, devastating fires sweep through Uttarakhand, Odisha, Chhattisgarh, and Madhya Pradesh, destroying biodiversity and threatening millions of livelihoods.

VanSuraksha uses deep learning and satellite data to detect fires before they spread, giving firefighters the critical time window to respond and protect these irreplaceable ecosystems.
